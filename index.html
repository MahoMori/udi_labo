Who We Are

Unnatural Death Investigation Lab is a laboratory specialised in investigating the cause of death.

About 400 postmortem examinations are conducted yearly at this facility.

We accept autopsy requests from police organisations, local authorities and individuals.


Several test labs are on-site within the building which help prompt determination of the cause of death.
